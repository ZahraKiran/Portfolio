About Me

I recently completed my Bachelors in Computer Science from SZABIST. I specialize in 3D modeling (Blender), Unity, and UI/UX. My FYP involved creating a zombie-survival game set in Karachi, where I modeled iconic landmarks such as Empress Market, Dolmen Clifton, Mazar-e-Quaid, Shaheen Complex, Quaid e Azam tomb, and Gazebo.
